Reliability for LLM Apps

Make model behavior predictable with boundaries, fallbacks, and visible failure states.

Principles

  • Boundaries: keep product logic outside the prompt.
  • Validation: validate inputs and tool outputs.
  • Fallbacks: have a safe response when confidence is low.
  • Observability: log retrieval results, latency, and refusal reasons.
