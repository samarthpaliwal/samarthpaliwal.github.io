Embeddings + Retrieval Quality

In many apps, chunking + metadata beats swapping embedding models.

The trap

Teams often try a new embedding model first. But retrieval usually fails because the chunks are too big, too small, missing metadata, or not aligned with user questions.

What actually moves the needle

  • Chunking: keep one idea per chunk; overlap when needed.
  • Metadata: add doc type, date, section title, product area.
  • Hybrid search: blend keyword + vector when language is specific.
  • Re-ranking: re-rank top-K with a stronger model.
